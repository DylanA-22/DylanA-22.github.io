Echo

What If?: Season 2 exceeded my expectations. Each episode was well directed, and considering the numerous opportunities that may come from the show and its premise of exploring the multiverse, the show is well written and manages to have a cohesive tale that binds each episode together in the end. The series delves into a variety of characters that were either side characters in the main MCU timeline or completely new characters. Whether it was a hero or a villain, the characters were well-written, and I was quite invested in it. I enjoyed the show a lot more than the first season and am very excited for season three.
