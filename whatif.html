Echo 1/23/24

The Echo show, starring Alaqua Cox as Maya Lopez, was a solid performance. When compared to the other MCU Disney Plus shows, I believe it ranks near the bottom. Maya Lopez as Echo is a solid addition to the Hawkeye show, but I didn't feel invested enough in her character to warrant a spin-off. While watching the show, I thought her relationship with her father, Kingpin, was interesting. As an anti-hero, almost a hero, I'm not sure how she fits into the MCU. One thing that piqued my interest was how the show tied in with the original Netflix shows, specifically Daredevil, played by Charlie Cox. I enjoyed the fight scene between the two, and now that it has been announced that those Netflix shows are part of the MCU, I'm curious how The Defenders will play a role. I thought Echo was a bridge to make those shows canon, and the story was okay to follow, so it gets 2.5 stars out of 5.
